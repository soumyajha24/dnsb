Our Story

Built on Trust, Driven by Purpose

Delhi Nagrik Sehkari Bank Ltd. was established in 1969 by our Founder Late Sh. T.C. Gupta — a visionary well-known in India's Co-operative Sector.

"The main objective of the Bank is to service the downtrodden and economically weaker sections of the society."

For over five decades, DNSB has been striving to fulfil the mission laid down by its founder — making banking accessible, inclusive, and trustworthy for every citizen of Delhi.
